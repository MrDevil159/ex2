Is Ketamine safe?

Ketamine infusion for depression has, thus far, been found to be extremely safe and without significant side effects. It is used at doses lower than what are used for anesthesia. >

You may be a candidate for ketamine infusion if you suffer from depression, certain anxiety disorders, Posttraumatic Stress Disorders (PTSD), or fibromyalgia that has not responded to other treatments. >
